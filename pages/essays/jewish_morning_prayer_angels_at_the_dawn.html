Morning Prayer — Angels at the Dawn

A prayer of gratitude, renewal, and angelic guidance.

"Modeh ani lefanecha - I give thanks before You,
living and eternal King,
for restoring my soul to me this morning.
Great is Your faithfulness.
Send Your holy angels before me today -
Michael with strength on my right,
Gabriel with courage on my left,
Raphael to heal every worry within me,
Uriel to light the path ahead.
May I walk this day with hope in my heart,
knowing Your angels go with me
in every step, every word, every moment.
Blessed are You, Lord, who renews creation every morning. Amen."
